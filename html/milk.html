Milk Quality Analyzer

Problem Statement

Ensuring the quality of milk is a major challenge for farmers, as traditional methods are often expensive and time-consuming, making it difficult to maintain consistent quality control.

Abstract

This affordable device detects the fat and SNF (Solid Not Fat) content in milk, providing an easy-to-use solution for quality control. The Milk Quality Analyzer aims to offer a budget-friendly method for farmers to analyze and maintain milk quality, ensuring better marketability and consumer trust.
