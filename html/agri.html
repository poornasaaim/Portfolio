Agribot

Problem Statement

Farmers often struggle with inefficient irrigation practices and lack precise information on soil conditions, leading to reduced crop yields and wasted resources.

Abstract

Agribot is an IoT and deep learning-based system designed for precision farming. It provides real-time soil data, including moisture, pH, and temperature, and offers crop recommendations based on this data. The system aims to optimize agricultural practices, leading to increased crop yields and sustainable farming.
